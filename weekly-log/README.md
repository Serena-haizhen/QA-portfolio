# Weekly Log

This folder contains my weekly learning retrospectives.

Every Sunday, I write a short reflection covering:

- **What I actually finished this week** — concrete outputs, not just hours spent
- **What got harder than expected**
- **What got easier than expected**
- **One thing I'll change for next week**

The goal is not to look impressive — it's to track real progress over time and stay honest with myself about what's working and what isn't.

## Structure

- `week-01.md` — first week
- `week-02.md` — second week
- ... and so on
