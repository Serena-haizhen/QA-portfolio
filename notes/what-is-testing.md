# What Is Testing

## What testing is
Software testing checks whether software works correctly, meets requirements, and helps reduce release risk.

## Why testing matters
Testing helps find problems before release and gives quality information to the team.

## What may happen without testing
Without testing, defective products may go live and cause customer loss, trust issues, and financial risk.

## The value of a tester
A tester does not prove a product is perfect. A tester helps the team understand product status, known issues, and potential risks.
