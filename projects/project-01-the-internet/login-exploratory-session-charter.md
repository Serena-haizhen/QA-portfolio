# Login Exploratory Session Charter

## What I will test
I will explore the login functionality, focusing on input behavior and system feedback.
I want to observe whether the login request is handled correctly after different user inputs.

## What I suspect may break
I suspect issues may appear with empty input, invalid input, special characters, incorrect access being allowed, and unclear error messages.
I also suspect the page feedback may not be consistent for different input combinations.

## What I will mainly observe
I will mainly observe page behavior, login feedback, error messages, validation messages, and redirect results.
I will also pay attention to whether the system reaction is consistent across different input combinations.
